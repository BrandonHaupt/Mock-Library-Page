Strange Magic: A Why Choose Paranormal Academy Romance

by Kate Nova

When Elsie finds herself transported into an unusual world of magic, she knows it’ll take more than basic spell-work to survive. She’s a magical misfit; a witch without a pedigree and an orphan. Three strikes against her and it’s only day one at the Academy. What begins as a strange adventure into a world where nothing is quite as it seems, quickly turns into a race to unlock the closely guarded secrets of her past before it’s too late. Strange Magic is the gripping first story in the Misfits of Magic Series. If you enjoy stories filled with enchanting creatures, otherworldly monsters, shifters, and slow-burn, why choose reverse harem romance, this is the series for you!
